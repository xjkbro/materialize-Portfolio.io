Hello there! My name is Jason-Kyle and I'm a Front End Web Developer.

I started my development journey in middle school learning HTML3 through a web design elective. In high school, I moved on to learning Java for two years and took the Computer Science AP Exam. I wasn't too sure about my career path at that point in time an was really interested in Computer Engineering. I entered the University of Nevada Las Vegas to focus on Computer Engineering. Later to find out that the field wasn't for me and that I actually missed programming, thus I switched my major to Computer Science. I learned many Object-Oreiented languages such as C++ and Java however, the ciriculum only focused on aspects that I wasn't to interested in. I'm a creator and artist. My whole life I've always been creative and took on many aspects of art as a hobby like photography, drawing, cinematography, digital design, etc. I wanted to create something visually appealing that my University's curriculum didn't have. So I sought out to become a web developer. I took it upon myself to learn from scratch January of 2019. While I still have a lot to go, my interest in Web Development sure won't.
